FAQ

Which Windows versions does it support?

Xylez Tweaks is built for Windows 10 and Windows 11 (64-bit). It runs as administrator to apply system tweaks — Windows will show a standard permission prompt on launch.

Can I undo the tweaks?

Yes. Every tweak is an on/off toggle you can revert individually, and the app can create Windows restore points so you always have a full rollback path.

How does the license work?

One key = one account = one PC. Your license is locked to your machine's hardware, so keys can't be shared. If you upgrade your PC, open a ticket in our Discord and we'll reset your key to the new hardware.

How do I get the app after paying?

Instantly. As soon as your payment goes through, your license key and the download link are emailed to you automatically — card or PayPal, same thing. If the email doesn't show up, check your spam folder first, then open a ticket in our Discord and we'll sort it.

Will this boost FPS in every game?

Results depend on your hardware and setup — a fresh high-end PC gains less than an older or cluttered one. The tweaks reduce background load, latency sources and bad defaults; most players feel the difference, but we don't promise magic numbers.

Is my Windows install safe?

The tweaks change standard Windows settings — the same ones you could change by hand in the registry or settings. Everything is reversible, and restore points are there as a safety net. A Windows update may occasionally reset some tweaks; just re-apply them.
